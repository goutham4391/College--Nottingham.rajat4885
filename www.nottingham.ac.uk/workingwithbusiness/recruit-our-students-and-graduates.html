Recruit our students and graduates

Recruiting sharp minds can bring fresh thinking and insights to your business. Our students are highly-sought after by some of the world’s top companies, innovative start-ups, and third-sector organisations.

Contact the Careers and Employability Service
 

Who do you want to recruit?

Graduates

Advertising your graduate vacancies

Whether you have one vacancy or 100, to be filled now or after graduation, advertise your roles for free through MyCareer – our online jobs board.

Get in touch to find out more about MyCareer, our recruitment fairs, employer presentations and how best to promote your opportunities and enhance your workforce.

Contact Recruiter Services - recruiterservices@nottingham.ac.uk 

Specific graduate programmes available

If you’re looking for a graduate to work on a specific project, we offer three programmes that could be benefit your business:

  • Nottingham Internship Scheme – for SMEs or charitable organisations, there’s funding available to recruit graduates for two to 10 weeks starting between September and November
  • Postgraduate Placements Nottingham – full-time three month placements for all businesses
  • Knowledge Transfer Partnership – a 12 to 36 month funded partnership between businesses, academics and researchers.

For more information, please contact Matthew Vincent on matthew.vincent@nottingham.ac.uk

 
Students for short-term projects

For short-term projects, our students can bring a fresh perspective through work experience or an internship. 

Advertising your vacancies

Whether it’s a holiday placement or a year-long position, advertise work experience or internship roles for free on MyCareer – our online jobs board – or through our recruitment fairs.

Contact Recruiter Services - recruiterservices@nottingham.ac.uk 

Specific student programmes available

There are other ways you can tap into the knowledge and expertise of our students:

Short term schemes

  • Hire students on a short-term basis, from up to 10 hours a week, or for two to 10-week placements during the Easter or summer vacations. Specific programmes include the Nottingham Internship Scheme and Postgraduate Placements Nottingham. There’s funding available to support you hiring our students on a short-term basis – contact us to find out if you’re eligible.
  • Nottingham Consultancy Challenge - a multidisciplinary team of five students work over six weeks during term time to produce a report and presentation on an issue identified by your business or charity.
  • Digital Marketing Academy – recruit a student to work on a digital marketing project part-time over four to eight weeks during term time.

Year-long schemes

  • Students from the majority of degree disciplines can take a year out to work on a year-long work placement with your organisation. We can help you recruit the right student.

For more information on the above schemes, please contact Matthew Vincent at matthew.vincent@nottingham.ac.uk

 
Students / graduates for part-time / temporary roles

If you’re looking to recruit students or graduates for part-time, temporary roles, get in touch with the Unitemps team – part of the Careers and Employability Service.

With a huge database of student and graduate candidates actively looking for flexible-work opportunities, we can help you find the right person for the role.

To recruit through Unitemps, please contact our team at recruit.unitemps@nottingham.ac.uk or call 0115 748 4189.
