Facilities and equipment

From testing laboratories to scientific equipment, our facilities are supported by researchers who can guide you through set-up and analysis.

Find out more 

Our facilities are spread across our faculties and support our key research areas.

If you know what you’re looking for, please contact the appropriate team and they will be happy to help. 

Alternatively, you can see most of our equipment listed on the National Equipment Database. 

View our internal directory (for University of Nottingham staff).
