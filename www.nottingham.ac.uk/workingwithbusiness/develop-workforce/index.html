Verified by UoNO 

University of Nottingham Online (UoNO) partners with organisations to offer external oversight and review of their learning products. 

We recognise these partnerships by issuing 'verified by' digital badging, certificates and transcripts to any learner who completes a learning product that we have reviewed and recommended for verification.
