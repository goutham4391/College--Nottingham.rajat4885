Collaborative funding

Explore the range of finance options available to help you work with our academics, graduates and facilities.

If you collaborate with us, there are funding opportunities to help support your business, from research and development to recruitment. Talk to our team to explore the options available.

Innovate UK funding

You can access between £25,000 to £10 million in grants to support collaborative research projects.

Go to Innovate UK funding



Knowledge Transfer Partnerships

A three-way collaboration between a business, an academic and a suitably qualified graduate to find innovative ways for businesses to grow.

Go to Knowledge Transfer Partnerships



Postgraduate Placements

Access up to 200 hours of high-calibre postgraduate students working directly in your business.

Go to Postgraduate Placements



Productivity through innovation

A programme of support for SMEs in Nottinghamshire or Derbyshire. Work with our postgraduate students through placements to identify and deliver productivity improvements in your business.

Go to Productivity through innovation



Funding for energy projects

If your business is based in Nottinghamshire, Derbyshire oe Leicestershire, you can access funding for energy projects supported by the European Regional Development Fund.

Go to Energy for business

 

Driving research and innovation

Nottinghamshire and Derbyshire SMEs can access support for innovation projects accessing our engineering, manufacturing, chemistry and food science capabilities.

Go to Driving research and innovation

 

Aerospace Unlocking Potential

Business support to SMEs in the aerospace supply chain throughout the Midlands. Access grants of up to £100,000 for innovation projects.

Go to Aerospace Unlocking Potential

 

Immersive technologies for SMEs

A wide-ranging programme of business support to introduce you to key concepts, technologies and case studies around immersive technologies.

Go to Immersive technologies for SMEs
