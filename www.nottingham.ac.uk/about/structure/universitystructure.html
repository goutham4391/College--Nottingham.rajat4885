The University is governed by two main bodies: Council and Senate.  At the head of the University are the Chancellor and Vice-Chancellor, Professor Shearer West.

The University's administrative and support departments support the running of the University and contribute to its growth and success both nationally and internationally. 

The University has a published Operating Model.
